# Engagement Tracking - FocusHub
FocusHub tracks engagement without invasive monitoring.

## Signals Captured
- Active watch duration
- Interaction frequency
- Quiz response speed
- Learning continuity

## What is NOT used
- No camera-based tracking
- No biometric monitoring
- No personal surveillance

## Purpose
Engagement signals are used to:
- Quantify learning focus
- Detect distraction patterns
- Improve personalization accuracy
