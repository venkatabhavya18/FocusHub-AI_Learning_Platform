# Verified Content Interaction Flow
FocusHub follows a curated content ingestion model.

## Content Upload Policy
Learning videos can be uploaded only by:
- Verifid faculty members
- Approved subject matter experts
- Trusted global educators

## Verification Process
- Educator identity is verified before upload access
  Content is reviewed for :
  - Curriculum relevance
  - Concept accuracy
  - Academic integrity

## Student Interaction
- Students cannot upload videos
- Students can only search and learn from verified content
- This prevents misinformation and learning confusion.

This ensures FocusHub remains a guided academic ecosystem, not an open-content platform.
